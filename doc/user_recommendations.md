# User Recommendations & Best Practices for Tejas

We highly recommend using a **dedicated email address** (e.g., `yourname.jobs@gmail.com`) specifically for your job applications.

- **Why?** This significantly reduces "false positives" (non-job related emails being incorrectly flagged) and keeps your personal inbox clutter-free.
- **Bonus:** It makes it easier for you to mentally separate job hunting from your daily life.
